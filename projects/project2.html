Overview

Rough Patch is a fast-paced top-down action game set in a haunted dollhouse. Trapped inside, players must fight through waves of possessed teddy bears, navigate deadly traps, and confront a powerful final boss in order to escape. The game blends tight, responsive combat with tense environmental hazards to create a uniquely eerie and challenging experience.

Developed as a collaborative college project with Matt Sinclair and Vannia Ung, Rough Patch showcases our combined creative and technical strengths. I was responsible for programming, composing the soundtrack, and co-leading level design. Matt focused on core gameplay systems and room layouts, while Vannia crafted the original art and brought the dollhouse to life through character and environment design.

Gameplay

  • Navigate a series of interconnected dollhouse rooms filled with enemies, traps, and puzzles.
  • Attack using a directional melee strike, and unlock a ranged boomerang attack partway through the game.
  • Use a directional dash to evade enemies or leap over spike traps and pits.
  • Solve light puzzles by hitting switches and collecting keys to unlock doors and progress.
  • Pick up heart-shaped patches to restore lives and stay in the fight.
