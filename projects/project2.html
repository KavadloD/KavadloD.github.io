Rough Patch

Rough Patch is a fast-paced top-down action game set in a cursed dollhouse. As the player, you're trapped inside and must battle through waves of sentient teddy bears, evade deadly traps, and overcome a powerful final boss to escape. The game combines sharp combat with tense environmental hazards for a uniquely eerie experience.

Developed as a college project in collaboration with Matthew Sinclair and Vannia Ung, the game showcases our combined talents. I handled the coding, music, and co-led level design, while Matthew focused on game design and level layout. Vannia brought the world to life with all-original art and character design.

Rough Patch was an opportunity to experiment with tone and tension while still keeping gameplay tight and satisfying. Each room of the dollhouse was designed to feel like its own little trap ‚Äî encouraging quick reflexes and smart movement.
