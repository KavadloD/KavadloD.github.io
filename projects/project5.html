Squash, The Bug

Unity · C# · Game Jam

Squash, The Bug was created for the Out of a Hat Jam, a game jam I organized and conceived as president of Maryville University’s Game Design Club. I came up with the jam’s concept: participants randomly drew a game mechanic, genre, and art style to inspire their game.

Our team drew Spin Dash as the mechanic, Platformer as the genre, and Arts & Crafts as the aesthetic—which led to the creation of Squash, The Bug.

You play as Squash, a tiny beetle with a big attitude who can roll into a high-speed ball to zoom through hand-crafted levels. With everything made of paper, cardboard, ink, and glue, the world of Squash is a vibrant diorama brought to life.

How to Play

  • Move and jump through platforming challenges using the arrow keys.
  • Hold Shift to roll up and launch forward using your spin dash ability.
  • Use your momentum to break through obstacles and bounce off enemies!
  • Navigate each level with speed, style, and a little bit of bug attitude.

Development Notes

  • Game jam duration: 1 week
  • All visuals were made to resemble real-world craft materials
  • Collaborative design, original programming, and level building
  • Led the jam event and contributed as a developer
