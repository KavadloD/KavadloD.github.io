Gameplay

  • Explore above-ground levels by running and jumping as Seedboy.
  • Interact with root points to dig underground and enter root mode.
  • Guide your roots in a snake-like mini-game through tight spaces to reach a goal.
  • Complete the underground path to unlock gates and new areas on the surface.
