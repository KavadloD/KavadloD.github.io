Overview

Saplink is a hybrid puzzle-platformer created in under 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2023. Based on the theme ‚ÄúRoots‚Äù, the game follows Seedboy, a curious little sprout on a journey of growth through layered challenges that shift between surface and underground gameplay.

The game was developed by a team of eight from the Maryville Game Developers Club. At the time, I was vice president and helped lead the project from start to finish. It was one of our most collaborative efforts, bringing together designers, programmers, artists, and writers in a fast-paced creative setting.

On the surface, Saplink plays like a classic platformer where you jump between ledges, avoid hazards, and explore nature-inspired levels. At key moments, you can dig into the ground, switching to a snake-style mini-game where you guide roots through narrow soil tunnels to unlock new areas.

Gameplay

  • Run and jump through above-ground levels as Seedboy.
  • Interact with root points to dig into the earth and enter root mode.
  • Guide your roots through a snake-like mini-game to reach the goal.
  • Completing the underground section opens gates and unlocks new areas.
