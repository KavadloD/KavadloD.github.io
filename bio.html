About Me

Hi, I’m Dorian Kavadlo — a QA and software developer with a background in game development, programming, and IT support. I earned my BFA in Game Design from Maryville University, where I studied interactive design, level development, and Unity programming. I am currently purusing my Matsers degree in Software Development, with an estimate graduation of Fall 2025.

While at Maryville, I served as President of the Game Developers Club. I organized game jams, launch parties, and dev meetups — giving students a space to build and share games together. I led team-based projects, built prototypes, and helped others grow their creative skills.

I’m working toward a career in QA or software development. I enjoy debugging, problem-solving, and ensuring great user experiences. Whether it’s a polished game mechanic or a clean UI, I love building things that look nice and feel good to use.
