About Me

Hi, I’m Dorian Kavadlo — a QA and software developer with a passion for game development, design, and music. I hold a BFA in Game Design from Maryville University, where I studied interactive design, level development, and Unity programming. I recently completed my Master’s in Software Development, further strengthening my skills in QA, programming, and creating polished, user-focused experiences. Alongside development, I compose original music and soundtracks for games, blending technical and creative expertise to bring projects to life.

At Maryville, I served as President of the Game Developers Club, where I organized game jams, launch parties, and meetups that gave students a space to create and share their work. I led team projects, built prototypes, and supported others in growing their creative skills.

I’m now pursuing a career in QA and software development, with a focus on debugging, problem-solving, and delivering great user experiences. Whether it’s a polished game mechanic or a clean UI, I enjoy building things that look good and feel even better to use.
